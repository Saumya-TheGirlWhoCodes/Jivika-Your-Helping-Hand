About Us

We facilitate the process of spreading awareness in a more convenient way. This is how we do it-

    We protect the identity of the person who seeks help. We work according to the user's convenience.

    For us, user's protection and mental health is foremost.

    We provide awareness and preventive measures to keep you safe and sound about the on-going crimes.

What extra milestone we cover?

We make the process of being informed and updated more transparent to you with some extended facilities.

  • 01 Analyzing Crime Rate in a Particular area

  • 02 NGOs and other helpful organizations

    We have also provided a list of NGOs and organizations on our website which can help you reach out to the right people for help.

  • 03 Try to reach the user's expectations

    We try to understand the user's expectations and try to be more and more supportive for them by covering each and every sector of on-going crimes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions that might help you in building a better understanding-

  • Does this website access my location?

    No, we are not using any feature which requires your location, thereby we do not access your location and your privacy is fully maintained.

  • How do you help me as an individual?

    We want to create awareness amongst all the users. This platform has been built so that people can get the knowledge of every crime and why is it caused and what all we can do to curb it. As an individual, you can be a part of this by spreading awareness and by helping each other.

  • Does this website provide me with the solution to problems like mental harassment and the ways in which I can prevent that?

    This website has a special chatbot named JIVIKA, that helps you to have a detailed overview of Emotional abuse (Crime against Women and Children) and Mental harassment (Crime against Women and Children) and the solutions and preventions of these issues are also mentioned clearly.

  • How do you help in the betterment of the society?

    This website is basically a platform for the Help where we try to help each and everyone by providing them with the TOLL FREE NUMBERS and the details of the organizations so that everything is accessible for each and everyone. We believe that “It is ok to ask for help” and promote this mentality.

  • Do you provide the solutions and preventions too along with the causes of the crime?

    Solutions and preventions are the key components of the website and they are mentioned for each and every crime category in the website. You can know more about it in the awareness section or can also ask our chatbot JIVIKA who is always happy to help.
